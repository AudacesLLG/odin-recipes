Chicken Katsu Recipe

Description

Chicken katsu is a Japanese-style fried chicken. While this recipe is made for Chicken Katsu, this recipe can be used to make Tonkatsu by using pork cutlets instead of chicken. Serve it with white rice and Tonkatsu sauce.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Seasonings
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Panko bread crumbs
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Season the chicken, then dredge in flour.
  2. Coat each breast in egg, then press into the Panko.
  3. Fry the chicken katsu until golden brown.
