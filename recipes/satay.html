Indonesian Chicken Satay

An Original Indonesian Chicken Satay recipe by Bunda Estherlita Suryoputro

Description

This recipe consist of Skewered Chicken Satay and a Mixed Peanut Sauce
This recipe took a Total Time of 1 Hours and yield a Total of 6 Servings

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp Soy Sauce and Tomato Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Peanut and Vegetable Oil
  • 2 cloves Garlic, peeled and minced
  • 1 pinch Ground Black Pepper and Cumin
  • 6 pcs skinless, boneless Chicken Breast halves-cubed
  • 1/2 cup Chunky Peanut Butter and Minced Onion

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, tomato sauce, peanut oil, garlic, black pepper, and cumin. Place chicken into the mixture, and stir to coat. Cover, and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 15 minutes, but not overnight. This will make the meat too dark.
  2. Preheat the Grill for High heat.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a saucepan over medium heat, and saute onion and garlic until lightly browned. Mix in water, peanut butter, and soy sauce. Cook and stir until well blended. Remove from heat, and set aside.
  4. Lightly oil the grill grate. Thread chicken onto skewers, and discard marinade. Grill skewers about 5 minutes per side, until chicken juices run clear. Serve with the peanut sauce.
